Summer School & English Language Immersion

English, culture and Ireland—experienced together.

A meaningful programme that connects communicative English learning with Irish culture, activities and time to explore.

English through experience

Language that is practical, engaging and connected to life.

Ireland 360° offers communicative English classes in small groups, led by qualified native teachers. Lessons focus on speaking, listening, confidence and fluency.

Project-based learning connects English to culture and everyday life, helping students use language in meaningful, real-world situations.

Culture & activities

Discover Irish culture beyond the classroom.

A rich cultural programme brings students together through creative, active and place-based experiences.

  • Traditional music, dance and storytelling
  • GAA experiences including hurling and Gaelic football
  • Local history, heritage tours, creative arts and drama workshops

Ireland to explore

From heritage cities to the edge of the Atlantic.

Visits can bring students into Irish towns, castles and cultural landmarks, with destinations including Dublin, Galway, Kilkenny and the Cliffs of Moher.

Across the Wild Atlantic Way, Ancient East, Copper Coast and Hidden Heartland, each programme can give students a memorable sense of Ireland through place, people and shared experience.
